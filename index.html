Services

Healthcare


expands access to essential medical services, particularly in remote areas, through mobile clinics, maternal care, and public health education. We aim to improve the health and well-being of Afghan communities by providing quality healthcare where it's needed most.

Cultural


fosters cultural enrichment by promoting activities that celebrate Afghan heritage, strengthen community bonds, and preserve cultural traditions. We empower individuals to connect with their roots and enrich their lives through cultural initiatives.

Social


drives socio-economic development by offering microfinance, vocational training, and job creation programs. We focus on empowering vulnerable populations, helping them achieve self-reliance and economic stability for a brighter future.

About Us

SOAD was established in 2015 and obtained its operating license from the Ministry of Economy of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan under resolution number 3508. Since its inception, the organization has been committed to enhancing the well-being of Afghan communities by focusing on cultural, social, and health sectors. SOAD strives to collaborate with the global community to promote sustainable development and improve the quality of life for the people of Afghanistan.

SOAD envisions a future where every Afghan thrives with access to quality healthcare, economic opportunities, and cultural heritage. We empower individuals and promote resilience through collaborative efforts that drive positive, lasting change across Afghanistan. By providing accessible healthcare, fostering economic growth, and celebrating culture, we address unique community challenges with innovative solutions. Through partnerships and a community-centered approach, we aim to build a brighter future where all Afghans can thrive.
